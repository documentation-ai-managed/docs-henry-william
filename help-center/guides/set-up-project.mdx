## Get Started

Easily create and organize your documentation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click "New Project" from your dashboard.
    Enter a name and description for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation Pages">
    Use the editor to add new pages for your docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Team Members">
    Go to "Settings" > "Team" and invite collaborators by email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You're ready to manage your documentation!
