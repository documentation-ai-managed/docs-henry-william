## Symptoms

- Login fails or page reloads
- "Invalid credentials" message

## Quick Fixes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    Click "Forgot Password?" at login and check your email for a reset link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Account Activation">
    Ensure you have verified your email before signing in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
