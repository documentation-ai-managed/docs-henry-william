## Overview

To use Henry William, you need to create a personal account.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://henrywilliam.com/signup`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign Up"
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the link to activate your account

## Next Steps

After activating, you can log in and set up your first documentation project.
